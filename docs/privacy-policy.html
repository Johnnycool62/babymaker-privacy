Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Baby Maker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Baby Maker does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The photos you upload are sent directly to our secure API for processing and are never stored on our servers.

2. Photos and Images

Photos uploaded by users are transmitted securely to generate AI predictions. These photos are processed in real time and are immediately discarded after the prediction is generated. We do not store, share, or use your photos for any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

Baby Maker uses the following third-party services to power its features:

  • Anthropic Claude — used to analyze parent photos. Images are processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI DALL-E — used to generate baby images. Images are processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — used to manage subscriptions. Data is handled according to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

All payments are processed through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription management is handled through RevenueCat.

5. Children's Privacy

Baby Maker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
